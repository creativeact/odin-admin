Gum Gum Pistol

Gum Gum Pistol

Luffy stretches his arm back like a rubber band and launches it forward in a powerful punch. The elasticity of his arm allows it to reach long distances and strike with significant force, making it one of his most frequently used attacks.

Gum Gum Bazooka

Gum Gum Bazooka

Luffy stretches both of his arms back and then slams them forward simultaneously, hitting his target with open palms. This attack combines the force of two powerful blows and can send opponents flying, showcasing Luffy's raw power and elasticity.

Gum Gum Gatling

Gum Gum Gatling

Luffy rapidly stretches and contracts his arms to deliver a flurry of punches, mimicking the rapid fire of a machine gun. This move overwhelms his opponents with the sheer number and speed of the punches, making it difficult for them to defend against.

Gum Gum Balloon

Gum Gum Balloon

Luffy inhales a large amount of air, inflating his body like a balloon. This makes him much larger and bouncier, allowing him to deflect attacks and even bounce back projectiles. It can also be used offensively to surprise and crush opponents.

Gum Gum Net

Gum Gum Net

Luffy stretches his fingers and interlocks them to form a net-like structure with his hands. He uses this net to restrain his opponents, preventing their movement and making it easier to land attacks. The elasticity of his fingers allows him to cover a wide area, making it difficult for targets to escape.

Second Gear

Second Gear

Luffy pumps blood through his body at an accelerated rate, boosting his physical abilities to superhuman levels. This transformation enhances his speed, strength, and agility, allowing him to move so fast that his body emits steam, giving his attacks a fiery appearance.
